How a cycle works
1

Fairlaunch

Fixed price for 24h. Bots can't snipe — everyone enters at the same price. Vote on the cycle's name, ticker and logo while you buy.

2

Bonding curve

Price moves with time, not market cap. The longer the cycle runs, the more it costs to enter. Trade freely — leaderboard updates live.

3

Graduation

Cycle ends, token auto-lists on Raydium. 50% of all fees route to the COOP airdrop. Then the next cycle begins. One token at a time.
